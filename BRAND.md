## Descriptions

### Short (≤150 chars — for AlternativeTo, social, app stores)

> Free calculators for construction, home improvement, and DIY. 73 calcs across electrical, plumbing, HVAC, carpentry, masonry, and finance.

### Medium (~500 chars — for BetaList, Product Hunt, IndieHackers)

> ProjectCalc is a free library of 73 calculators for construction, home improvement, and DIY projects — used by contractors estimating jobs and homeowners planning weekend projects. Coverage spans carpentry (beam span, stair stringer), electrical (voltage drop, panel load), plumbing (drain & vent sizing), HVAC (Manual J, BTU), and home & DIY (concrete, drywall, paint, mulch, gravel). Every calculator shows the formula, worked example, and rules of thumb. Includes a unique visual room sketcher that pre-fills calculators with your dimensions. No signup, no popups, no ads. Runs entirely in your browser.

### Long (~1000 chars — for site directories, press, longer-form listings)

> ProjectCalc is a free library of 73 calculators for construction, home improvement, and DIY projects. Built solo with Next.js and deployed on Vercel, it covers the math contractors and homeowners actually need: electrical voltage drop and conduit fill (NEC), plumbing drain and vent sizing (IPC), HVAC Manual J and duct CFM (ACCA), structural beam span and snow load (IRC + AWC), masonry brick counts and rebar grids, plus everyday material math like concrete bags, drywall sheets, paint gallons, mulch yards, and tile counts. Every calculator shows the formula, a worked example, common mistakes, and rules of thumb so users can sanity-check the result. A unique visual room sketcher draws the user's room as they type dimensions and pre-fills the matching calculator with the numbers — handles L-shaped rooms with corner cutouts. No signup, no popups, no AI chatbot, no email capture. All calculations run entirely in the browser; inputs never leave the page. Code references documented to NEC, IPC, IRC, ACCA, ACI, and ASCE.
